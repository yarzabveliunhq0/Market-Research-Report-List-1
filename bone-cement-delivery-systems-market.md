<p><p>The Bone Cement Delivery Systems market is segmented into three categories based on the viscosity of bone cements: high, medium, and low viscosity. High-viscosity cements offer excellent support for load-bearing applications and are often used in spinal surgeries. Medium-viscosity cements provide a balance of workability and strength, making them suitable for a variety of orthopedic procedures. Low-viscosity cements, being more fluid, are typically used for intricate applications and to facilitate easier flow into bone cavities during surgery.</p></p>
